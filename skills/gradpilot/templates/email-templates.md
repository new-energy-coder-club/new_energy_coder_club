# 套磁与沟通邮件模板

> 所有模板需根据具体情况个性化修改，避免直接复制。

---

## 模板 1：首次套磁（PhD / 研究型硕士）

**Subject**: Prospective PhD Student — [Your University] — [Research Area, e.g., NLP / Robotics]

```
Dear Professor [LastName],

I am [Your Name], a senior undergraduate student in [Major] at [University], 
expected to graduate in [Month Year]. I am writing to inquire about potential 
PhD opportunities in your group starting [Fall 20XX].

I have been following your work on [Specific Topic], particularly your recent 
papers on [Paper Title / Project Name] ([Conference/Journal, Year]). Your 
approach to [Specific Method/Insight] resonates strongly with my research 
interests in [Your Area].

During my time at [University/Lab], I have built a foundation in [Relevant Skills]
through [Project/Research Experience]. Specifically, I [Describe Your Contribution 
with Metrics, e.g., developed an X that achieved Y% improvement on Z dataset]. 
These experiences have equipped me with [Specific Techniques/Tools] and deepened 
my commitment to [Research Direction].

I would be grateful for the opportunity to discuss whether you are planning to 
accept new students for [Fall 20XX]. I have attached my CV for your reference 
and would welcome the chance to learn more about your group's current projects.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your University]
[Email] | [Personal Website / LinkedIn, if applicable]
```

## 模板 2：跟进套磁（无回复，2 周后）

**Subject**: Follow-up: Prospective PhD Student — [Your University]

```
Dear Professor [LastName],

I hope this message finds you well. I am writing to follow up on my email from 
[Date] regarding PhD opportunities in your group.

I understand you receive many such inquiries and appreciate your busy schedule. 
If you are available, I would still welcome any feedback on my fit with your 
research or information about recruitment for [Fall 20XX].

Thank you again for your time.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

## 模板 4：延期 Deposit Deadline

**Subject**: Request for Deposit Extension — [Your Name] — [Program Name]

```
Dear Admissions Office / Program Coordinator,

I am writing to request an extension for the enrollment deposit deadline. 
I am truly honored to have been admitted to the [Program Name] at [University].

However, I am currently awaiting decisions from a few other programs to which 
I applied, as I want to make a fully informed decision regarding my graduate 
studies. I expect to hear back by [Specific Date].

Would it be possible to extend my deposit deadline to [Requested Date]? I am 
very excited about the opportunity to join [University] and appreciate your 
consideration.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Application ID, if available]
```

**Subject**: Update on My Application — [Your Name] — [Application ID]

```

[University], where I was recently placed on the waitlist.

Since submitting my application, I have [New Achievement / Update, e.g., 
completed a new research project / improved my GRE score / received a paper 
acceptance]. I believe this further demonstrates my commitment to [Field] and 
my readiness for the rigorous training your program offers.

[University/Program] remains my top choice because [Specific Reason]. I would 
be thrilled to accept an offer if one becomes available.

Thank you for your continued consideration.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

## 邮件礼仪要点

1. **称呼**：确认教授职称（Dr. / Professor），不确定时用 "Professor"
2. **时间**：避开周末和节假日，工作日早上 9-11 点发送最佳
3. **附件**：CV 必附；可附成绩单、论文首页（视情况而定）
4. **签名**：包含全名、学校、邮箱、LinkedIn/个人主页
5. **跟进**：无回复 10-14 天后可跟进一次；仍无回复则放弃，不要 spam
